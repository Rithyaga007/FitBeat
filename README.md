# FitBeat
FitBeat - AI wellness classification based recommendation system
The rapid growth of wearable fitness devices, wellness applications, and AI-powered health platforms has transformed the modern wellness industry by enabling continuous monitoring of user activity and lifestyle behavior. However, most existing fitness platforms primarily focus on activity tracking and fail to provide personalized behavioral insights capable of improving long-term user engagement and wellness consistency. This project presents an AI-Driven Behavioral Wellness Classification and Personalized Recommendation System designed to analyze wearable fitness and lifestyle data and generate intelligent wellness recommendations based on individual behavioral patterns.

The proposed system integrates Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, behavioral analytics, and recommendation systems into a unified digital wellness platform. The system analyzes multiple wellness indicators including daily steps, active minutes, calorie burn, sedentary behavior, workout consistency, sleep quality, stress levels, and activity patterns. Based on these behavioral attributes, users are classified into wellness categories such as Highly Active Users, Sedentary Users, Weekend Warriors, and Inconsistent Users. Machine Learning models including Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, and Random Forest were implemented and evaluated for behavioral wellness classification, where the Random Forest model achieved the highest classification accuracy.

A major highlight of the project is the AI-driven recommendation engine, which generates personalized wellness suggestions, activity improvements, and product recommendations tailored to individual user behavior and wellness goals. The system also demonstrates strong business value by supporting behavioral segmentation, personalized marketing strategies, customer engagement, and ROI-focused wellness solutions for fitness and wellness brands. The findings of the study demonstrate the practical application of Artificial Intelligence in creating intelligent, personalized, and behavior-aware wellness ecosystems capable of improving both user wellness engagement and business growth.
